About Us

Holy Spirit Fishgen Church Trece, located in Trece Martires City, Cavite, Philippines, is a prominent place of worship affiliated with the Born Again movement.

  • Started

    In 1997, Pastor Vidal and Pastora Rhoda Day Carag were sent to Trece Martires City, Cavite, where they began the work of the Lord by holding Sunday services in their own home, with only their family members attending – the pastoral couple and their two children.

  • Thriving

    Today, FISHGEN Trece is a strong and thriving congregation, considered a blessing to the community for its continued and passionate sharing of God’s Word in schools, universities, offices, and even among the police force.

  • Community Engagement

    By God’s grace and faithfulness, the ministry of Pastor Vidal and Pastora Rhoda has also borne fruit – establishing one FG branch (FISHGEN Osorio) and three outreaches located in Santiago and San Francisco, General Trias, and Silang, Cavite.
