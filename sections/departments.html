Ministry

Our ministries are designed to support spiritual growth, fellowship, and service within the church.

  • Program
  • Worship
  • Media
  • Dance
  • Usher & Marshal

Program

The Program Ministry mobilizes members to plan and lead worship, events, and outreach—turning faith into action through teamwork and service.

Rooted in service, it mobilizes members—young and seasoned—to organize worship services, community events, and spiritual workshops. From coordinating music and liturgy to leading small-group studies and outreach projects, the ministry fosters teamwork, discipleship, and a tangible expression of the church'''s mission. Its heartbeat? Turning love into service, one purposeful program at a time.

Worship

The Worship Team leads worship through song, guiding choirs, instrumentalists, and the congregation to create an atmosphere of praise and spiritual connection.

It nurtures budding musicians, mentors seasoned artists, and orchestrates everything from Sunday praise to special festivals, ensuring the soundtrack of faith resonates deeply across the community.

Media

The Media Ministry is the digital heartbeat of the congregation, broadcasting sermons, live-streaming worship, and crafting videos, podcasts, and graphics that reach people beyond the sanctuary.

It manages social platforms, sound and lighting tech, and ensures the church's message travels clearly to both local and global audiences. By turning faith into visual and audio content, it connects, educates, and invites all to experience the community's life.

Dance

The Dance Ministry uses movement to express worship, joy, and the story of faith.

Through choreographed performances, liturgical dance, and creative workshops, it brings a visual, kinetic dimension to services, special events, and community outreach. The ministry nurtures talent, fosters worshipful artistry, and invites the congregation to celebrate God’s love through the beauty of dance.

Usher & Marshal

The Church Usher and Marshal Ministry serves as the welcoming front line, guiding worshippers, managing seating, and ensuring smooth flow during services and events.

Ushers greet attendees, provide assistance, and maintain order, while marshals coordinate logistics, coordinate volunteers, and handle any security needs. Together, they create a hospitable, organized environment where the congregation can focus on worship and community.
